AASTU Peer-to-Peer Academic Exchange
🚀 Bridging the Gap in Ethiopian Engineering Education
AASTU Peer-to-Peer is a specialized academic marketplace designed specifically for the students of Addis Ababa Science and Technology University. Engineering and Applied Science problems often involve complex models and drawings that standard AI tools struggle to interpret. This platform connects students who need help with "solvers" who have mastered the material, creating a sustainable reward-based ecosystem.

🛠 Features
Departmental Categorization: Dedicated sections for all AASTU majors:

Engineering: Mechanical, Electrical, Software, Mining, Environmental, Civil, Mechatronics, and Chemical.

Applied Sciences: Food Science, Industrial Chemistry, and Biotechnology.

Pay-to-Unlock Model: Integrated payment system using Ethiopian Birr (CBE, Telebirr, etc.) to ensure solvers are rewarded for their expertise.

AI-Assisted Verification: An integrated AI layer that analyzes submitted solutions to ensure accuracy and relevance before they are finalized.

Visual Problem Solving: Built to handle high-resolution images and technical drawings that traditional LLMs often fail to parse.

🏗 System Architecture
The platform is designed to handle secure transactions and high-traffic during assignment deadlines.

Student Posts Question: Uploads text and images to a specific department.

Solver Submits: A student solves the problem and uploads the solution.

AI Validation: The system runs a preliminary check on the solution logic.

Transaction: The requester pays a small fee in ETB to unlock the full solution.

Payout: The solver receives their reward, incentivizing high-quality academic help.
